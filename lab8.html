Designing for All: Accessibility and Web Design Best Practices

Designing for All: Accessibility and Web Design Best Practices emphasizes the importance of creating websites that are inclusive and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities.
By following accessibility guidelines and best practices, web designers can ensure that their sites are navigable by screen readers, keyboard-only users, and those with visual impairments.
Prioritizing accessibility not only improves user experience but also ensures compliance with legal standards, making the web more open and equitable for all users.
